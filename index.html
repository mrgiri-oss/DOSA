About

WHAT WE STAND FOR ...

We bring together Odia students across Delhi to celebrate our culture, support one another, and create a strong, united community away from home.

The Delhi Odia Students Association (DOSA) is a student-led community that connects Odia students across colleges and universities in Delhi. We create a space where culture, identity, and friendships thrive beyond classrooms.

From celebrating festivals like Raja and Durga Puja to hosting cultural events, networking sessions, and mentorship initiatives, DOSA connects students to their roots while helping them grow personally and professionally.

We believe in community, collaboration, and continuity — building friendships that last beyond college and creating a strong Odia presence in Delhi.

Our Objectives

1

Foster Community

Build a strong and welcoming network for Odia students across Universities in Delhi.

2

Help students adjust, grow, and thrive to life in Delhi through guidance and mentorship.

Encourage participation through events, discussions, cultural nights, and collaborative activities.

Promote Odia culture and pride among students in Delhi.
